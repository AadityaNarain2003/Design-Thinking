Research Goals:

1.To understand the impact of academic pressures on the well-being and academic performance of undergraduate students.

2.To assess how neglecting personal well-being leads to a decline in academic performance.

3.To explore the role of institutional support systems in managing academic stress and promoting well-being.

Research Objectives:

1.To identify key stressors contributing to academic pressure among undergraduate students.

2.To analyze time management strategies and their effectiveness in managing academic stress.

3.To evaluate the role of institutional support systems in improving student well-being and academic outcomes.
